The works of my hand, Max Kapambwe

Welcome to Max Kapambwe's portfolio website. Here you will find various creative works I has worked on as well as fragments of my upbringing and how I became the man I am today.

A brief list of some creative works I have tried

  • Drawing: Did alot of drawing when I was younger, it was my way of expressing what I couldn't with words and keeping myself entertained.
  • Board games: I made a few board games when I was younger, nothing super original but I thought long and hard about how I would implement certain aspects of realtime games into them.
  • Story writing: Loved thinking of stories and drawing scenes from them, was terrible at writing, Didn't stop me from trying though.
  • Music: I made some music in my later teenage years, mostly Lofi like, video game type music.
  • Video games: Started making video games when I was around 16 years old,First ever game I "finished" was BioTech.
